Last updated: May 2026

INFORMATION WE COLLECT
The App does not require an account and does not collect personally identifiable information directly. However, third-party services integrated in the App may collect certain data:

- Apple Game Center – leaderboard participation is optional and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
- Analytics (if applicable) – aggregate, anonymized usage data may be collected to improve the App. No data is sold or shared with advertisers.

DATA STORAGE
Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device or in your iCloud account via Apple's standard mechanisms. We do not operate our own servers to store your personal data.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.
