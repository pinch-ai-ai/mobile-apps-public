Why You'll Love It

Features

🎮

Simple Controls

Tap four buttons to move Elon across shelves. Pick up in seconds, hard to put down.

📈

Progressive Difficulty

Play starts with one ball and scale up to four simultaneous balls as your score grows. Stay sharp!

🔊

3-Mode Sound System

Off, basic, or full effects — cycle with a tap. Rich sounds: catches, drops, whooshes, and a sad trombone on game over.

🏆

High Score Tracking

Your best "Budget Saved" is auto-saved between sessions. Can you beat your own record?

🎱

Branded Balls

Unique ball-logo designs appear at random. Catch them all!

🥚

Easter Eggs

Hidden surprises are waiting to be discovered. You'll know when you find one.
